Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

1. Introduction

At CollegeBazaar, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our platform.

This policy applies to all services offered by CollegeBazaar, including our website, mobile applications, and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from our users:

  • University email address and verification status
  • Profile information (name, student ID, major)
  • Listing details and transaction history
  • Communications with other users
  • Feedback and support requests

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Verify your student status and maintain platform security
  • Enable transactions between users
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure compliance with our terms

We never sell your personal information to third parties or use it for purposes other than those specified in this policy.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Access controls and authentication

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data in a portable format

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing
  • Email communications
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • User verification

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and can only use it for specified purposes.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through:

  • Email notifications
  • Platform announcements
  • Website notifications
